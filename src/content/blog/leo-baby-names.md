Leo, the fifth sign of the zodiac, is the radiant ruler of the astrological kingdom. Born between July 23 and August 22, Leo babies are known for their natural confidence, generous hearts, and magnetic charisma that draws people in like moths to a flame. Ruled by the Sun — the center of our solar system, the source of all warmth and light — and belonging to the fire element, Leo children are born to shine. Choosing a name for your little lion means selecting one that carries the weight of royalty, the warmth of sunshine, and the courage of the king of beasts.

The lion symbolizes Leo, representing strength, nobility, courage, and natural authority. Famous Leo personalities like Barack Obama, Jennifer Lopez, and Madonna embody the sign's trademark blend of star power and generous warmth. The 50 names in this guide celebrate themes of lions, royalty, gold, sunlight, and bold leadership — each one a crown-worthy choice for a Leo child.

> **Key Takeaway:** Leo baby names should reflect the sign's royal, sun-kissed nature. Look for names meaning lion, king, queen, gold, or sun. For more regal inspiration, explore our guide to [baby names meaning fire](/blog/names-meaning-fire/) and the timeless name [Oliver](/blog/name-oliver/), which carries strength and noble roots that Leo admires.

## Understanding the Leo Personality

Leo is a fixed fire sign, meaning these children are steady in their warmth and unwavering in their confidence. They are natural performers who love to be seen, celebrated, and adored — but they give back just as much love and attention as they receive. Key Leo personality traits include:

- **Confident and charismatic** — Leo children command attention the moment they enter a room, often with a dazzling smile.
- **Generous and warm-hearted** — They share their toys, their hugs, and their laughter with breathtaking generosity.
- **Creative and dramatic** — Expect a child who loves dressing up, performing, and making everything a grand production.
- **Loyal and protective** — Leo children are fiercely devoted to their friends and family.
- **Natural leaders** — They organize games, delegate roles, and inspire others to follow their vision.

### Leo Element and Ruling Planet

The fire element gives Leo their passion, creativity, and vibrant energy. Fire signs live boldly and expressively. The Sun, the ruling celestial body, adds warmth, vitality, and a natural center-of-attention quality. Names connected to the sun, gold, lions, kings, queens, and light all resonate with Leo energy.

## Naming Tips for Your Leo Baby

### Go Regal and Bold

Leo is the sign of royalty, so do not be afraid to choose names that sound grand and commanding. Augustus, Cleopatra, Leopold, and Regina carry the kind of regal weight that Leo wears naturally. These names are not pretentious on a Leo — they are a perfect fit for a child who will naturally command respect and attention.

### Connect to the Sun

The Sun rules Leo, making solar-inspired names particularly powerful. Apollo (god of the sun), Aurora (dawn light), Cyrus (sun), Samson (sun child), and Solana (sunshine) all carry the brilliant warmth of Leo's ruling star. Sun names suggest a child who lights up every room they enter. For more names connected to celestial light, see our guide to [baby names meaning star](/blog/names-meaning-star/).

### Choose Names with the Lion Connection

The lion is Leo's symbol, and names meaning lion carry the sign's essence directly. Leo and Leon are the most obvious, but Leona (lioness), Leopold (brave people), and Griffin (strong lord, associated with lion-like creatures) also honor the lion's courage and nobility. For more courage-inspired names, explore our collection of [baby names meaning brave](/blog/names-meaning-brave/).

## FAQ

### What are the best baby names for a Leo?

The best Leo baby names reflect the sign's royal, sun-ruled nature. For boys, consider Leo (lion), Arthur (bear king), Augustus (great, magnificent), and Apollo (god of the sun). For girls, look at Leona (lioness), Aurora (dawn light), Regina (queen), and Aurelia (golden). Names connected to lions, royalty, the sun, and gold perfectly capture Leo's magnificent energy.

### What names mean lion for a Leo baby?

Several names directly mean lion, making them natural Leo choices. Leo is the Latin word for lion. Leon is the Greek form. Leona and Leonie are feminine versions meaning lioness. Leopold means "brave people" with a lion root. Ariel means "lion of God" in Hebrew. Leonard means "brave as a lion" in German. Singh means lion in Punjabi and is commonly used as a name.

### What is the Sun's influence on Leo baby names?

The Sun rules Leo, making it the most radiant and confident sign. This solar influence favors names meaning light, gold, brightness, and warmth. Apollo was the Greek god of the sun. Cyrus means "sun" in Persian. Samson means "sun child" in Hebrew. Aurora means "dawn" in Latin. Solana means "sunshine" in Spanish. Sun-connected names give Leo babies an extra layer of celestial power.

### What are royal names suitable for a Leo baby?

Leo is the sign of kings and queens, making royal names especially fitting. For boys, Rex (king), Augustus (great), Henry (ruler of the home), Kingston (king's town), and Regis (kingly) all carry royal authority. For girls, Regina (queen), Victoria (victory), Cleopatra (glory of the father), Queenie (queen), and Zara (princess) offer regal elegance. These names honor Leo's natural-born leadership.
