## FAQ

### What name means happy for a boy?

Felix is the most direct Latin name meaning happy and lucky. Asher, from Hebrew, means happy, blessed, and fortunate. Isaac means he will laugh. Onni is a Finnish name meaning happiness. Bennett is a modern variation of Benedict, meaning blessed and happy.

### What girl name means blessed?

Beatrice means she who brings happiness and blessed in Latin. Gwyneth is a Welsh name meaning blessed and happy. Beata is a Latin name meaning blessed and beatified. Sachiko is a Japanese name meaning child of bliss. Felicity means intense happiness and good fortune.

### Are there cultural names meaning happy?

Yes, many cultures have beautiful happy names. Sachiko and Keiko come from Japanese. Tayo and Ayo are Yoruba names from West Africa. Tashi is a Tibetan name meaning prosperity and good fortune. Macario comes from Spanish and Greek roots meaning happy and blessed. Onni is Finnish for happiness.
