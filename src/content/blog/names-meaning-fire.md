Fire has been a symbol of passion, energy, and transformation since the dawn of civilization. Names that mean fire or flame carry an intensity and warmth that few other name meanings can match. They suggest a child who will burn bright, light up every room, and bring warmth to everyone around them. From the Irish Aiden to the Hindi Agni, fire names span the globe and carry centuries of cultural significance.

Fire-inspired names work for parents who want something dynamic and unforgettable. These names are neither quiet nor subtle — they announce themselves with energy and confidence. Yet many fire names also carry undertones of warmth, comfort, and protection, like the hearth fire that draws a family together. The 35 names below offer a blazing selection for every taste.

> 📌 **Key Takeaway:** Fire and flame names are bold, energetic, and full of passion. They come from every major language and culture, offering parents a wide range of fiery options from the gentle warmth of Ember to the blazing intensity of Blaze. For the ultimate contrast, pair this list with our collection of [baby names meaning ocean](/blog/names-meaning-ocean/) for a fire-and-water naming palette.

## The Element of Fire in Naming

Fire names can range from the literally fiery — like Blaze and Flame — to the more poetic, like Seraphina, whose Hebrew root means burning angel. Consider whether you want a name that immediately evokes fire or one where the flame connection is a deeper, more subtle meaning. Both approaches create names that carry warmth and intensity.

## FAQ

### What boy name means fire?

Aiden is the most popular boy name meaning fire, coming from the Irish word for little fire. Blaze is a direct English word name meaning flame. Ignatius means fiery one in Latin. Agni is the Sanskrit name for the Hindu god of fire. Keegan means son of fire in Irish.

### What girl name means fire or flame?

Seraphina means fiery ones in Hebrew and refers to the burning angels in biblical tradition. Bridget is an Irish name associated with the goddess of fire. Enya means little fire. Fiamma is the Italian word for flame used as a name. Pele is the Hawaiian goddess of fire and volcanoes.

### What are gentle fire names for babies?

Ember is a soft, warm name meaning a glowing coal — it suggests warmth without intensity. Kiran means ray of light in Sanskrit, carrying a gentle fire connection. Edana means little fire in Irish with a feminine, delicate sound. Soleil, meaning sun in French, evokes the warmth of fire without the harshness.
